  <h1>5-Day Gout-Friendly Weekly Exercise Plan</h1>
  <h2>Monday–Friday • Weekends Off</h2>

  <div class="day">
    <div class="title">Monday – Mobility + Light Cardio</div>
    <div class="section"><span class="label">Focus:</span> Ease into the week, joint mobility and heart health</div>
    <div class="section"><span class="label">Routine:</span>
      <ul>
        <li>5-min warm-up walk</li>
        <li>10 min mobility drills</li>
        <li>15 min walk or light cycling + stretching</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="section"><span class="label">Tip:</span> Keep posture upright and avoid walking if your foot or toe joint feels irritated.</div>
  </div>

  <div class="day">
    <div class="title">Tuesday – Full Body Strength (Bodyweight)</div>
    <div class="section"><span class="label">Focus:</span> Build joint-supporting muscle safely</div>
    <div class="section"><span class="label">Routine:</span>
      <ul>
        <li>2–3 rounds of:</li>
        <li>10 chair squats</li>
        <li>10 wall push-ups</li>
        <li>10 seated knee raises</li>
        <li>10 step-ups</li>
        <li>30s plank or wall hold</li>
        <li>2–5 min cooldown walk</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="section"><span class="label">Tip:</span> No jumping or floor exercises; keep all moves controlled.</div>
  </div>

  <div class="day">
    <div class="title">Wednesday – Cardio + Stretch</div>
    <div class="section"><span class="label">Focus:</span> Increase circulation, lower uric acid</div>
    <div class="section"><span class="label">Routine:</span>
      <ul>
        <li>20–30 min brisk walk or bike</li>
        <li>5–10 min full-body stretching (calves, thighs, hips, shoulders)</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="section"><span class="label">Tip:</span> Try swimming if accessible — excellent joint-friendly cardio.</div>
  </div>

  <div class="day">
    <div class="title">Thursday – Strength + Balance</div>
    <div class="section"><span class="label">Focus:</span> Maintain joint integrity and reduce fall risk</div>
    <div class="section"><span class="label">Routine:</span>
      <ul>
        <li>10 bodyweight squats</li>
        <li>10 seated leg lifts</li>
        <li>10 resistance band rows</li>
        <li>30s single-leg stand (2–3x)</li>
        <li>10 wall push-ups</li>
        <li>5 min cooldown stretch</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="section"><span class="label">Tip:</span> Use a chair for balance if needed during single-leg work.</div>
  </div>

  <div class="day">
    <div class="title">Friday – Recovery Flow / Gentle Yoga + Walk</div>
    <div class="section"><span class="label">Focus:</span> Recover, relax, and stay limber</div>
    <div class="section"><span class="label">Routine:</span>
      <ul>
        <li>15 min gentle yoga (cat-cow, folds, side stretches)</li>
        <li>10–15 min easy walk</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="section"><span class="label">Tip:</span> Don’t overdo it — this is an active recovery day.</div>
  </div>
