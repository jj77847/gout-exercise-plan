Gout-Friendly Exercise Tracker PWA app looks like and how it works:

App Overview

  • Clean, minimal, and mobile-friendly UI

    The interface is designed to be easy on the eyes with plenty of white space, clear headings, and color-coded day cards to help you quickly identify your workouts.

  • Color-coded day cards for Monday to Friday

    Each weekday has a distinct border color to visually separate days:

    • Monday: Red

    • Tuesday: Orange

    • Wednesday: Yellow

    • Thursday: Green

    • Friday: Blue

  • Weekly views displayed in a vertical scroll

    You see all 4 weeks’ workouts stacked from top to bottom, each day clearly labeled with its name and week number.

  • Each day card includes:

    • Day name and week number as a heading

    • Focus description (e.g., “Mobility + Light Cardio”)

    • A bulleted list of exercises for that day

  • Interactive Pie Chart summary at the bottom

    A colorful pie chart shows your total exercise volume per day (over all 4 weeks). This helps you quickly spot which days have heavier or lighter workloads.

  • Offline ready & installable

    Because it’s a PWA, you can install it on your phone or desktop. Once installed, you can open it without internet, making it perfect for the gym, park, or anywhere.
